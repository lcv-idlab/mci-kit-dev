Title: Gaze Sonification

----

Subtitle: A sonification device to mediate Sophie Taeuber-Arp’s visual compositions

----

Authors: Selena Savic / Gabriele Gambotto / Peter Law

----

Abstract: 

The concept of the project is to create an interactive sonification of Sophie Taeuber-Arp’s ‘Horizontal-Vertical Composition’ for all visitors (blind, partially-sighted and sighted).

(link: https://vimeo.com/205063178 text: Video of the project)
